About

Sweet and Sour is a podcast dedicated to capturing the common threads and the nuance of the Asian American experience. In our episodes, we'll discuss topics such as activism, education, relationships, food, family, media representation, language, Asian Americans' relation to other minorities, etc. Expect 15-25 minute episodes every two weeks.

Sweet and Sour is made by katie zhu and Nicole Zhu, two sisters living on opposite coasts.
